Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Batoucada ("we", "us", or "our") built ZooShift as a free application. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. ZooShift works entirely offline and does not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information. There are no analytics, cookies, or advertising SDKs.

Game Data

Your game progress (scores, stars, completed puzzles) is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data, and it is never transmitted anywhere.

Internet Permissions

ZooShift does not require internet access. The app functions completely offline.

Third-Party Services

ZooShift does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

ZooShift is designed for children and families. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, there is nothing to disclose, delete, or manage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at contact@batoucada.fr.
