UNJAM

A sliding puzzle game inspired by Rush Hour. Free the red car in as few moves as possible.

Get it on Google Play

Features

🧩

100 Puzzles

5 difficulty levels from Beginner to Expert. Unlock new challenges as you progress.

🎯

Drag & Drop

Smooth, intuitive controls with spring animations and real-time collision detection.

💡

Smart Hints

Stuck? The built-in BFS solver finds the optimal solution instantly.

⭐

3-Star Rating

Solve puzzles in fewer moves to earn stars and track your best scores.

↩️

Undo & Reset

Made a wrong move? Undo up to 50 moves or reset the puzzle entirely.

🏆

Player Profile

Track your stats, completion rate, and overall progress across all levels.
