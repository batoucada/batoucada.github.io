Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Batoucada ("we", "us", or "our") built the UNJAM app as a free application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

UNJAM is a fully offline puzzle game. It does not require an internet connection and does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.) is collected.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No device identifiers are collected.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No cookies or similar technologies are used.
  • No advertising SDKs or ad networks are integrated.

Game Data

Your game progress (completed levels, star ratings, and move counts) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Internet Permissions

UNJAM does not request or use internet permissions. The app operates entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

UNJAM does not use any third-party services that collect information. There are no analytics services, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks embedded in the app.

Children's Privacy

UNJAM does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
